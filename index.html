Services

What I deliver

Shop Drawings Review
Consultant-level review of electrical shop drawings — delivering clear, actionable technical comments ensuring full compliance with project specifications and international standards.
SLD & Load Calculations
Single Line Diagram review and load calculation verification — ensuring correct sizing, diversity factors, and full compliance with IEC and local utility standards.
Short Circuit & Protection Studies
Fault level analysis, protection coordination studies, and relay setting review — ensuring system safety and selectivity from source to final circuit.
MAR / Submittals Review
Material Approval Request and technical submittal review at consultant level — verifying conformance with specifications, data sheets, and project requirements.
MEP Coordination
Multi-discipline MEP coordination — resolving clashes between electrical, mechanical, and plumbing systems to accelerate approvals and protect programme.
